## Motivation

Author consistency is widely recommended in quality/E-E-A-T guidance for
human search systems. Its effect on LLM entity confidence is empirically
under-documented in public experiment form.

### Facts

- The same person can appear with divergent titles, bios, and images across
  domains.
- [`sameAs`](https://schema.org/sameAs) and identical [`Person`](https://schema.org/Person) properties are common consistency mechanisms.
- Primary public surfaces for this subject: [barbu.es/about](https://barbu.es/about/), [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/barbugeorge/), [GitHub](https://github.com/george-barbu-es), [InfoWebPlus](https://infowebplus.com/).

### Assumptions

- “Entity confidence” can be proxied via: answer decisiveness, inter-model
  agreement, contradiction rate, and willingness to state the affiliation
  without hedging - while treating hedging as informative, not purely negative.

### Unknowns

- Whether photo consistency matters for text-only LLM probes (may matter more
  for multimodal or KG pipelines).
- How many domains are required for a detectable effect.

### Identity kit under test (intended)

| Signal | Consistency target |
| --- | --- |
| Photo | Same canonical headshot URL or identical file hash lineage |
| Bio | Same short bio string (±minor locale tweaks logged) |
| Titles | Controlled vocabulary (e.g. Founder) |
| Company | Canonical organization string (InfoWebPlus) |

Subject: **George Barbu**, Founder of **InfoWebPlus**, with related property
**barbu.es**.

---

## Hypothesis

**H₀:** After aligning photo/bio/title/company signals across domains, AI
confidence proxies and affiliation accuracy do not improve vs baseline
inconsistency.

**H₁:** Consistency alignment increases affiliation accuracy and reduces
cross-domain identity contradictions in AI answers.

---

## Methodology

### Design type

Before/after consistency remediation across a declared domain set.

Baseline deliberately documents inconsistencies as Facts (do not “clean”
before capturing baseline).

## Variables

### Independent variables

- Cross-domain consistency level (low → high) across photo, bio, titles, company

### Dependent variables

- Affiliation accuracy (“works at / founded”)
- Contradiction rate across platforms in a single answer
- Hedging frequency (coded, not automatically “bad”)
- Inter-model agreement on title + company

### Controlled / held constant

- No new major PR campaigns during the window if avoidable
- No change to unrelated entities’ names

### Confounds to monitor

- Schema deployment (Exp-002) shipping simultaneously
- Social network graph changes
- Homonym risk

---

## Procedure

1. Inventory all public author appearances; score consistency 0-1 per signal.
2. Run baseline probes emphasizing identity questions.
3. Remediate to the identity kit; log each URL changed.
4. Re-probe; compare DVs; report per-signal ablations if resources allow
   (bio-only vs photo+bio, etc.).

### Prompt protocol

```text
Who is George Barbu? Where does he work?
What is George Barbu’s role at InfoWebPlus?
Are there conflicting identities for George Barbu online? What conflicts do you see?
Provide a short author biography for George Barbu suitable for a research byline.
```
